# ADR-005: Inbound BD Strategy Over Outbound Discovery

**Date:** March 29, 2026 (Sprint Day 40)
**Status:** Accepted
**Decision:** Pivot from outbound token discovery to inbound BD funnel

**Context:**
Outbound discovery scanned DexScreener trending, Bags.fm (168K tokens), CoinGecko
rank 300-600, and Colosseum hackathon projects. Result: 0 tokens scored above 70/100
with honest tri-source verification.

**Decision:**

- Sunday Listing Intelligence Report at buzzbd.ai/report as primary BD funnel
- "DM @BuzzBySolCex for your score" CTA drives inbound interest
- ELS-1 proposal at buzzbd.ai/proposal attracts protocol-level partners
- Outbound discovery continues via ARIA but as LEAD GEN, not direct outreach

**Consequences:**

- No wasted outreach to tokens that don't qualify
- Projects that find us are self-selecting (they want to be scored)
- Honest scoring is the VALUE PROPOSITION (most tokens fail — that's the point)
- Revenue depends on inbound volume, which takes time to build
